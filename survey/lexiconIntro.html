Introduction to Verbal probabilities
Verbal probabilities or uncertainty expressions are linguistic terms or phrases used to convey the likelihood or degree of certainty about a statement or event without using numerical values.
These expressions are often employed when quantifying uncertainty or probability is difficult or when precise numerical data is unavailable or unnecessary.

Example of verbal probability in a sentence:
"With clear skies and rising temperatures, it's unlikely that we'll see any precipitation for the rest of the week."
